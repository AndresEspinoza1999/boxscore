Privacy Policy

Benchscore ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you create a Benchscore account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for account authentication only
  • Username — publicly displayed on your reviews
  • Favorite sport — used to personalize your feed

When you use the app, we also store:

  • Game reviews and ratings you submit
  • Games you add to your watchlist (saved games)
  • Likes you give to other users' reviews

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to operate Benchscore. Specifically:

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To display your reviews and username to other users
  • To personalize your home feed based on your favorite sport
  • To maintain your watchlist and liked reviews across sessions

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not serve ads.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database provider. Supabase stores data on servers in the United States. By using Benchscore, you consent to your data being stored there.

Live game scores and schedules are fetched in real time from ESPN's public API. We do not store ESPN data permanently — it is only used to display current scores.

4. Data Retention

Your account and all associated data (reviews, watchlist, likes) are retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

5. Children's Privacy

Benchscore is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Sign in with Apple

If you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive a unique identifier and, optionally, your name and email address from Apple. Apple may anonymize the email address shared with us. We use this information only to create and manage your Benchscore account.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your reviews and ratings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Benchscore after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
